# Templates

GRC (Governance, Risk & Compliance) documents that AI can help complete or review. Templates are structured documents — DPIAs, checklists, risk registers, processor agreements — pre-formatted for use with Claude or another LLM.

## How to use a template

1. Navigate to the relevant regulation subfolder.
2. Read the `README.md` in the template folder for instructions.
3. Follow the instructions to use the template with an AI assistant.

## Regulation subfolders

| Folder | Coverage |
|---|---|
| [`/gdpr/`](./gdpr/) | GDPR / AVG — data protection impact assessments, processor agreements, privacy notices |
| [`/ai-act/`](./ai-act/) | EU AI Act — system classification, conformity assessments, risk registers |
| [`/nis2/`](./nis2/) | NIS2 Directive — cybersecurity obligation checklists, incident reporting templates |
