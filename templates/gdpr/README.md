# GDPR / AVG Templates

Compliance documents for GDPR obligations — ready for AI-assisted completion or review.

## Planned templates

| Template | Description | Status |
|---|---|---|
| DPIA (Data Protection Impact Assessment) | Structured DPIA template per Article 35 GDPR | Coming soon |
| Data Processing Agreement (DPA) | Processor agreement template per Article 28 GDPR | Coming soon |
| Privacy Notice | Layered privacy notice template per Articles 13–14 GDPR | Coming soon |
| Records of Processing Activities (RoPA) | Article 30 GDPR processing register | Coming soon |
| Data Breach Notification | Internal breach register + notification template (Articles 33–34) | Coming soon |
