# EU AI Act Templates

Compliance documents for EU AI Act obligations (Regulation (EU) 2024/1689) — ready for AI-assisted completion or review.

## Planned templates

| Template | Description | Status |
|---|---|---|
| AI System Classification Checklist | Risk classification per Article 6 + Annex III | Coming soon |
| High-Risk AI System Documentation | Technical documentation per Article 11 + Annex IV | Coming soon |
| Conformity Assessment | Self-assessment template for high-risk AI systems (Article 43) | Coming soon |
| Risk Register | Risk management system template per Article 9 | Coming soon |
| GPAI Model Documentation | General Purpose AI model documentation per Article 53 | Coming soon |
| Fundamental Rights Impact Assessment | FRIA template per Article 27 for deployers | Coming soon |
