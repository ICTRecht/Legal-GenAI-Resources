## How to use

1. Download the documents you need.
2. Upload them to your AI platform's knowledge base (e.g. Claude Project, OpenAI Assistant, or a custom RAG pipeline).
3. Use a Skill from [`/skills/`](../../skills/) as the system prompt, or write your own.
4. Test retrieval with realistic legal questions.

## Suggested demo scenarios

- **Privacy chatbot** — Load GDPR/AVG documents from [`/resources/gdpr/`](../gdpr/) and use the GDPR Compliance Analyst Skill
- **AI Act classifier** — Load AI Act text from [`/resources/ai-act/`](../ai-act/) and use the EU AI Act Navigator Skill
- **Contract reviewer** — Upload a sample contract and use the Legal Contract Reviewer Skill
- **Woo/open data assistant** — Load Woo documents from [`/resources/general/`](../general/)

## Contributing demo data

Only add publicly available documents. Do not upload documents containing personal data or confidential information.
